START-UP REGISTRATION

Register your business with us. Legal Serv helps in the entire process of registration required for your business to get running.

The services provided in Business Registration are:

Obtain Digital Signature Certificate (DSC): The first step is to obtain a DSC, which is a secure digital key used for signing electronic documents. You can obtain a DSC from any of the certified agencies in India. Obtain Director Identification Number (DIN): Next, obtain a DIN, which is a unique identification number for every director of the company. You can apply for DIN through the MCA portal by providing the necessary documents. Reserve Company Name: After obtaining a DSC and DIN, reserve a name for your company through the MCA portal. Ensure that the name complies with the naming guidelines prescribed by the MCA. File Incorporation Documents: Once the name is approved, file the incorporation documents such as Memorandum of Association (MOA) and Articles of Association (AOA) through the MCA portal. Ensure that all the necessary documents are attached and the fees are paid. Obtain Certificate of Incorporation: Once the documents are verified and approved, the MCA will issue a Certificate of Incorporation, which is the conclusive proof of the existence of the company. You can download the certificate from the MCA portal.

MCA registration refers to the process of registering a company with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) in India. It is a mandatory requirement for all companies operating in India. The documents required for MCA registration in India include the company's name, address, directors' details, articles of association, and memorandum of association. The process for registering a company with MCA in India involves obtaining a Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) for the directors, obtaining Director Identification Number (DIN), reserving the company name, drafting the Memorandum of Association (MOA) and Articles of Association (AOA), filing the incorporation documents, and obtaining the Certificate of Incorporation. Yes, anyone can register a company with MCA in India, provided they have all the necessary documents and follow the process as prescribed by the MCA. However, it is recommended to seek the assistance of a professional such as a chartered accountant or company secretary for a hassle-free registration process. The time taken to register a company with MCA in India varies depending on various factors such as the type of company, the availability of all documents, and the workload of the MCA. Generally, it takes around 7-10 working days for the registration process to complete. The cost of MCA registration in India depends on various factors such as the type of company, the authorized capital, stamp duty, and professional fees. Generally, it ranges from Rs. 5,000 to Rs. 15,000. Registering a company with MCA in India provides various benefits such as legal recognition, access to credit, limited liability protection, and compliance with the law. It also adds credibility to the company's name and provides a platform for growth and expansion. .

How to register for obtaining gst number for my business

Gather Documents: The first step is to gather all the necessary documents such as PAN card, proof of business registration, address proof, and bank account details. These documents are required for the registration process. Register on GST Portal: Next, register on the GST portal by providing your details such as name, PAN number, email ID, and mobile number. You will receive an OTP on your registered mobile number and email ID for verification. Fill the Application Form: After registration, fill in the GST registration application form by providing the necessary details such as business name, address, and bank account details. Upload the necessary documents and submit the form. Wait for Verification: Once the application is submitted, the GST officer will verify the details and documents provided. If any discrepancies are found, the officer may ask for additional information. If everything is in order, the GST officer will approve the registration and issue a GSTIN (Goods and Services Tax Identification Number). Download GST Certificate: Once the registration is approved, you can download the GST certificate from the GST portal. The certificate contains the GSTIN, legal name of the business, and the date of registration.

GST is a consumption-based tax that is levied on the supply of goods and services in India. It replaced multiple indirect taxes like VAT, Service Tax, etc. Any individual or entity whose turnover exceeds Rs. 20 lakhs (Rs. 10 lakhs for special category states) in a financial year is required to register for GST. The different types of GST returns are GSTR-1, GSTR-2A, GSTR-3B, GSTR-4, GSTR-5, GSTR-6, GSTR-7, GSTR-8, GSTR-9, and GSTR-9C.
